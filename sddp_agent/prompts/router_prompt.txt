## Classification rules
- "problema_convergencia" → Zinf/Zsup gap not closing, convergence criterion not met,
  too many iterations, cut count stagnation, policy did not converge
- "deslocamento_custo" → simulation cost systematically differs from policy cost,
  policy vs simulation mismatch, FCF copied from another case, non-convexities
- "problema_simulacao" → simulation quality, penalty proportions, MIP solver status,
  hourly simulation issues, ENA dispersion, operating cost dispersion, cost scatter
- "violacao" → violations, penalties too high/low, constraint breaches, infeasibility,
  soft constraint calibration, mean vs max violations, seasonal violations, constraint conflict
- "cmo" → marginal operating cost (CMO/CMg/LMP), zero prices, negative prices, supply surplus,
  price volatility, price dispersion across scenarios, spillage penalties driving CMO negative

When the question is ambiguous, prefer "problema_simulacao" for general quality questions.
When the question mentions violations, penalties (not convergence-related), or soft constraints, use "violacao".
When the question mentions marginal costs, zero prices, or price volatility across scenarios, use "cmo".

Return ONLY valid JSON (no markdown, no explanation outside the JSON).
